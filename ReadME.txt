Platform Used: Microsoft Power BI
Project Overview: Developed a comprehensive dashboard to visualize and analyze e-commerce sales, profitability, and performance metrics, providing actionable insights for business decision-making.
Key Features:
Sales Analysis: Monitored key metrics such as total sales amount (438K), profit (37K), and average order value (AOV 121K) to assess overall performance.
State-wise Sales Distribution: Visualized sales distribution across major states like Maharashtra, Madhya Pradesh, Uttar Pradesh, and Delhi.
Category Analysis: Displayed sales quantity breakdown by category (Clothing, Electronics, Furniture) to highlight top-performing product segments.
Payment Mode Analysis: Analyzed preferred payment modes (COD, UPI, Debit, Credit) for better understanding customer payment preferences.
Monthly Profit Trends: Tracked monthly profit trends to identify seasonality and peak sales periods.
Customer Segmentation: Visualized top customers based on purchase amount to identify high-value clients.
Sub-Category Profitability: Highlighted the profitability of sub-categories like Printers, Bookcases, and Sarees for targeted marketing strategies.
Tools & Technologies: Power BI, DAX, Data Cleaning, and Visualization Techniques.
Impact: Enabled better sales forecasting, targeted marketing strategies, and data-driven decision-making.
